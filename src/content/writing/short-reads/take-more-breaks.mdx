title: Take More Breaks
pubDatetime: 2026-01-12T15:00:00.000+05:30

The bug you've been staring at for an hour?

Take a walk. Get coffee. Look out a window.

Your brain keeps working in the background.

I can't count how many times the solution came to me:
- In the shower
- While making lunch
- Walking to the mailbox

Sometimes stepping away **is** the work.
